About Me

I am a Software Engineer and community advocate based in not-always-so-sunny San Francisco, CA. Since 2017, I have helped hundreds of people learn full-stack web development at freeCodeCamp meet-ups in San Francisco's Mission and SOMA districts and online in our Facebook group of over 4600 self-taught learners.


Previously, I was an Associate Instructor for General Assembly's flagship 13-week Software Engineering Immersive Program. In addition to teaching full-stack development, I am on the leadership team for Out In Tech U, a global LGBTQ+ mentorship program for young people pursuing careers in tech.


I believe everyone can equip themselves to make their lives and the world better. You can often find me at tech meet-ups in San Francisco, trying new boba drinks, and trying to meet my daily walking quota. I am non-binary and use They/Them pronouns.
